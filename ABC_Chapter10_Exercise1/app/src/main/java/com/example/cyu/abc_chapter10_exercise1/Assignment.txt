Case Project 10-1: Facial Expressions App

Requirements Document

Application title: Facial Expressions App

Purpose:
A series of images uses Frame animation to demonstrate seven facial expressions of an actor.

Algorithms:

1. The opening screen displays the first image of a man with a happy facial expressions
   (Figure 10-29).

2. When the user taps the SEE THE ACTOR button, the seven facial expressions are each displayed for
   two seconds. After each image is shown once, the animation ends.

Conditions:

1. The pictures of the seven facial expressions of the actor are provided with your student files
   with the names of face1 through face7.
