Android Boot Camp
Case Project 7-6: Personal Photo App

Requirements Document

Application title: Personal Photo App

Purpose:
Create your own photo app with eight images of your family and friends in a GridView control.

Algorithms:

1. Create a GridView that displays eight images of your friends and family.

2. When the user selects a specific thumbnail image in the GridView, a large image appears with
   a custom toast message that states the first name of the pictured person.

Conditions:

1. Select your own images.

2. Use a layout of your choice.
